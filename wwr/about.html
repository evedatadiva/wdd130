White Water Rafting Company

Welcome to Splash White Water Adventures, your premier destination for thrilling white water rafting experiences! Our mission is to provide unforgettable adventures, foster a love for the great outdoors, and promote environmental conservation. With our expert guides and top-of-the-line equipment, safety is our top priority as we navigate through exhilarating rapids and breathtaking landscapes. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-time adventurer, join us for an adrenaline-fueled journey down the river of a lifetime!

History

Splash White Water Adventures was founded in 1995 by outdoor enthusiast Evelin Flores. What started as a small family business offering guided rafting trips on local rivers quickly grew into a leading adventure tourism company. Over the years, we've expanded our operations to include trips on some of the most iconic rivers in the country, from the roaring rapids of the Colorado River to the scenic beauty of the Snake River. Our commitment to safety, professionalism, and environmental stewardship has earned us a reputation as the go-to choice for thrilling white water rafting experiences. Join us as we continue to share the excitement and wonder of river rafting with adventurers of all ages!
